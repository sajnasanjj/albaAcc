Audit & Assurance

Audit Related Service

Auditing is both a complex and crucial task. Proper auditing ensures the integrity of your business operations, helping you manage your accounts effectively and detect and prevent errors and fraud. Choosing the right audit firm is critical, as it can significantly impact your business’s future. Here’s what you can expect when choosing one of the best auditing firms in the UAE:

1. External Audit

External auditing involves verifying the accuracy of financial records and operations by independent auditors. This process ensures compliance with UAE regulations and helps address any issues or confirm the financial claims of the organization.

2. Internal Audit

Internal auditing provides objective assurance and adds value to an organization’s operations. We approach internal audits holistically, offering a systematic and disciplined approach to enhance the effectiveness of controls, governance processes, and risk management.

3. Tax Audit

A tax audit is an evaluation conducted by the government to determine a company's compliance as a taxable entity

4. Revenue Audit

The revenue audit consists of the review of the tax return, the inspection of the declaration of liability and the representation of liability in respect of the stamp duty, and the verification of the person's compliance with their obligations under the tax and duty legislation.

5. Interim Audit

An interim audit is similarly performed in response to the client's request, requiring their interim financial statements to be reviewed every quarter.

6. Investigative Audit

Also known as forensic auditing, investigative auditing involves a detailed examination to identify fraudulent activities and uncover missing funds. It combines extensive accounting expertise with investigative skills to provide comprehensive insights into financial discrepancies.

7. Due diligence Audit

Auditing is one of the major aspects of any financial procedure. It helps in dealing with reviewing, analyzing a process, or quality system. Auditing provides you the compliance of your requirements. Depending on the demand, auditing can be for an entire organization or a particular business function. It provides insights into the company’s true worth, helping attract investors by reflecting the growth and actual value of the business.

8. Business Valuation

It analyses the company’s competitiveness according to market competition, income values, and asset values. Through business valuation, best audit firms in UAE understand the resale value of a company. It obtains a true company value, showing your company’s actual value according to the growth of your business, and helping you access more investors. In short, professional auditing helps you as well as others understand the real value of your organization.

9. Business plan and feasibility studies

With extensive experience across various industries, we assist clients in preparing robust business plans tailored to their needs and budget. Our services include market analysis, feasibility reports, and demographic studies, ensuring comprehensive support for your business objectives.
